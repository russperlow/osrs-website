Our Vision

At Infuse RS, our aim is to create a helpful, mature community with a perfect balance of a laid-back social, as well as a PVM and skilling experience.

We are therefore looking for those with a mature and friendly outlook and all-round love of Oldschool Runescape, that can help us provide and deliver the Clan experience you have all been looking for.

Requirements

We value (pro)activity, maturity and fun. These are characteristic traits we therefore seek in members who apply. During your probation week, we require you to show these traits. Moreover, as of July 21, 2019 we require applicants to have a reasonably achievable total level of 1250.
